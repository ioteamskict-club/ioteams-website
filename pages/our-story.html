◆ How It Started

A Late-Night Idea That Became a Movement

In 2022, a group of five engineering students at IIUM Gombak stayed up late in the computer lab — not studying for exams, but figuring out how to make a sensor talk to the internet. That night, IoTeams was born.

What started as an informal study group quickly grew as more students discovered the joy of building connected devices. Within months, the group had a name, a committee, and official recognition from IIUM Student Affairs.

Today, IoTeams is one of IIUM's most active technical societies — running workshops, hackathons, industry talks, and collaborative projects that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world IoT applications.

◆ Milestones

Our Journey So Far

2022 — Q1

Founded by 5 Students

IoTeams officially formed in IIUM Gombak with 5 founding members and a shared passion for connected devices.

2022 — Q3

Official IIUM Recognition

Received official recognition from IIUM Student Affairs Division, enabling us to host official events and access university resources.

2022 — Q4

First Workshop — 40 Attendees

Hosted our first public workshop on Arduino basics. 40 students attended — far more than we expected. The demand was real.

2023 — Q2

First Hackathon — 12 Teams

Organised IoTeams' first internal hackathon. 12 teams competed over 48 hours to build IoT solutions for campus problems.

2023 — Q4

100 Members Milestone

Crossed 100 registered members — students from Engineering, IT, Science, and even Business who wanted to learn IoT.

2024 — Q2

Industry Partnership with Local Tech Firms

Established partnerships with Malaysian IoT companies for mentorship, project sponsorship, and internship opportunities for members.

2025 — Now

120+ Members & Growing

Today IoTeams runs 3 programme tracks, hosts regular events, and continues to grow as the go-to IoT community at IIUM.

◆ What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔧

Build First

We believe in learning by doing. Every concept is better understood when you build it with your own hands — even if it breaks the first time.

🤝

Community Over Competition

We lift each other up. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, everyone has something to teach and something to learn.

🌍

Real Impact

We build for the real world. Our projects tackle actual problems — smart campus, sustainable living, accessible technology.

📖

Open Knowledge

Everything we learn, we share. Our workshops, documentation, and project guides are open to all IIUM students.

⚡

Move Fast

Ideas are worthless without execution. We prototype quickly, iterate fast, and ship projects that actually work.

🕌

IIUM Spirit

We carry the values of IIUM — integrity, innovation, and excellence — into everything we build and every collaboration we form.
