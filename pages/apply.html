Common Questions

Is membership really free?

Yes — completely free. IoTeams is a student society supported by IIUM Student Affairs. There are no membership fees, and all workshops and sessions are provided at no cost to members.

I'm not from an engineering or IT faculty. Can I still join?

Absolutely. IoTeams is open to all IIUM students regardless of faculty. We have members from Business, Medicine, Law, and Architecture. IoT is interdisciplinary — every perspective brings value.

Do I need to know how to code before joining?

No coding experience is required for the Hardware & Embedded track. We start from the very basics. The Cloud and AI tracks benefit from some Python knowledge, but we provide resources to catch up.

Can I join more than one track?

Yes! Many members participate in multiple tracks. We recommend starting with one and adding more once you're comfortable. You can also select "All Tracks" in the application form.

How many hours per week does membership require?

Sessions are typically 2–3 hours once or twice a week per track. Beyond that, project work is self-paced. We understand academic commitments come first — there's no strict attendance requirement.

What equipment will I need to buy?

IoTeams provides all components and equipment needed for sessions through our equipment library. For personal projects, we can advise on affordable starter kits, typically RM50–RM150 for an ESP32 or Arduino setup.
