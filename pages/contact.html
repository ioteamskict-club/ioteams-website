Open in Google Maps
How to Get Here
1

Take the KTM Komuter or bus to Gombak Terminal.

2

Board the IIUM shuttle bus from Gombak Terminal (free for students).

3

Alight at the Kulliyyah of Engineering stop.

4

Head to Block E, Level 2. Look for the IoTeams sticker on the lab door.

Parking

Visitor parking is available at the main gate. Inform the guard you are visiting the Kulliyyah of Engineering. Parking is free for registered students.
